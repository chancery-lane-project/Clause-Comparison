	<h4>Construction : Procuring Materials and Carbon Budgets</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A formal ‘carbon budget’ alongside the traditional financial budget for construction projects, to incentivise industry participants to reduce greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions through use of more sustainable materials. To set a carbon budget for construction projects governed by JCT Design & Build Contract 2016 and reduce greenhouse gas emissions by incentivising parties to adopt more sustainable practices and materials.</p>
