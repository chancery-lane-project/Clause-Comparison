	<h4>Green Transaction Execution Protocols</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Green execution protocols which parties can adopt at the start of a transaction to minimise the carbon footprint of deal execution. Brings climate aligned practices and sustainable behaviours to financial and corporate deals: for example, reducing paper use, increasing recycling and minimising travel wherever possible.</p>
