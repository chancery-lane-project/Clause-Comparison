	<h4>Green Litigation and Arbitration Protocols</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Two similar protocols (litigation and arbitration) with modules which parties can opt into at the start of a dispute to make it lower carbon and greener. Provides practical ways for parties to litigation or arbitration to conduct disputes in more sustainable, climate-aligned ways.</p>
