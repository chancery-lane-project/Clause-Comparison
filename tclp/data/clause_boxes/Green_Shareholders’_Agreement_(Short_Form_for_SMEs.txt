A short form of Lauren’s Clause (Green Shareholders' Agreement) for small or medium enterprises (SMEs), enabling shareholders to embed alignment with Paris Agreement goals at the highest level of the SME, and prioritise them over short term fast growth to deliver better performance and long term value.
