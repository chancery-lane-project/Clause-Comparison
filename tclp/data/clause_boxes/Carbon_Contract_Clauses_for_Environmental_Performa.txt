	<h4>Carbon Contract Clauses for Environmental Performance, and Associated Incentives and Remedies</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A clause that sets climate and environmental performance metrics for suppliers, incentives for meeting supplier performance targets and consequences for breach. This clause incentivises suppliers to meet emissions-reduction targets set out in the contract by introducing remedies tiered according to the level of breach.</p>
