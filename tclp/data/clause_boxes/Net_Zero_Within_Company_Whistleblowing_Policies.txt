Long Name: Net Zero Within Company Whistleblowing Policies
Excerpt: Guidelines to incorporate wider environmental protections into companies’ whistleblowing regimes in line with their adopted policies on net zero. Introduces provisions to cover climate-related whistleblowing.. Many companies operate in the EU and are implementing the EU Whistleblowing Directive and re-evaluating their procedures. Therefore, it is timely to consider what environmental protections should be included in a company’s whistleblowing regime. By doing so, companies are demonstrating to their employees and other stakeholders that they are serious about their net zero commitments.
