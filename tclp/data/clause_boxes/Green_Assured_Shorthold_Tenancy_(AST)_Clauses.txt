	<h4>Green Assured Shorthold Tenancy (AST) Clauses</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Green AST clauses to give tenants a modest rental rebate for 'green' household management and give landlords the ability to invest in renewable energy installations while their rentals are occupied. Incentivises tenants to occupy a rental property in a sustainable way and allows landlords to install renewable energy solutions at the property while it is still occupied.</p>
