Child's Name: Lagatha's Clause
Long Name: Auditing Water Usage in Supply Chains
Excerpt: A clause aimed at reducing the use and wastage of water throughout a company's supply chain by requiring suppliers to provide audit results to the company. Requires suppliers to provide results of a water audit, which may be used to set performance targets and mitigate the environmental impact of water treatment and supply. 
Lack of visibility regarding water usage in a company’s supply chain can hinder achievement of its environmental and net zero targets.
