	<h4>Net Zero Aligned Construction Modifications</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A clause that incentivises building contractors to propose 'Net Zero Modifications' to project works, which will benefit the Employer, the overall Project and the environment into the future. Incentivise contractors and employers to provide more sustainable construction solutions during the delivery of the works by providing a mechanism for net zero modifications to the works.</p>
