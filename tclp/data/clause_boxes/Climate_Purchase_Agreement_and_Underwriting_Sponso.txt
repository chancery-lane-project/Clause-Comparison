	<h4>Climate Purchase Agreement and Underwriting Sponsor Warranties</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Extension of director and major shareholder warranties and issuer/director undertakings in underwriting, sponsor and similar agreements in respect of environmental position and climate risk. Parties to underwriting, purchase and sponsor agreements can use this clause to incorporate climate warranties and undertakings from directors with a view to continuous improvement. This allows companies to address their progress to achieve net zero targets, resulting in improved transparency, accountability and performance against climate risk and impact metrics.</p>
