	<h4>Climate Policy Footprint</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A checklist that requests one-off or repeated disclosure of a company’s climate-related lobbying, financing, sponsoring and climate leadership activities. Enables information to be obtained from a corporate counterparty to ensure that its activities are aligned with Paris Agreement goals.</p>
