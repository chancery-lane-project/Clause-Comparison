	<h4>Climate Aligned Construction Waste Management</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Contractual obligations in respect of the usage of materials and waste management to ensure consequential GHGs are minimised. Imports climate-aligned waste management practices into traditional and design and build construction contracts.</p>
