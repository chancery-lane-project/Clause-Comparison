Child's Name: Toby's Clause
Long Name: Avoiding Excessive Paperwork in Dispute Resolution
Excerpt: A contractual obligation between the parties stating that, in the event of a dispute, parties agree to settle it in a ‘green’ manner. This clause can be used in any commercial agreement and provides certainty that any dispute must be handled in a manner that reduces the use of paper. The clause also includes a commitment by both parties to offset emissions arising from any dispute and to consider how such dispute may be handled in a way that minimises greenhouse gas emissions.
