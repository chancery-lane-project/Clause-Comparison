	<h4>Avoiding Excessive Paperwork in Dispute Resolution</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A contractual obligation between the parties stating that, in the event of a dispute, parties agree to settle it in a ‘green’ manner. This clause can be used in any commercial agreement and provides certainty that any dispute must be handled in a manner that reduces the use of paper. The clause also includes a commitment by both parties to offset emissions arising from any dispute and to consider how such dispute may be handled in a way that minimises greenhouse gas emissions.</p>
