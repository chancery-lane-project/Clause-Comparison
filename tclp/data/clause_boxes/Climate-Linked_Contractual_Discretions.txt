	<h4>Climate-Linked Contractual Discretions</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Climate-linked contractual discretions creating scope for parties to encourage good environmental practice. Linking contractual decision-making and the exercise of powers to the other party’s performance against climate objectives may incentivise positive environmental business practices, thereby reducing emissions and speeding up the transition to net zero.</p>
