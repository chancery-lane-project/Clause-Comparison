Child's Name: Ragnar's Clause
Long Name: Green Company Articles
Excerpt: An underlying framework of green obligations, which target a company’s decision-making, internal investments and reporting in addressing material environmental issues for the company. Introduces ‘Green Articles’ demonstrating a clear corporate commitment to addressing environmental issues. This may give customers increased confidence in the company, generate new brand loyalty, attract business partners and may be helpful in achieving certain benchmarks for increased investment interest.
