	<h4>The ‘Green Supplier’ Contract – A Standardised Contractual Pricing Model</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A standardised green supplier schedule that helps purchasers to reduce Scope 3 emissions by incentivising their supply chain to adopt environmental targets. This schedule introduces an incentive-based model into supply agreements to ensure that sustainability objectives agreed between supplier and purchaser are adhered to and maintained.</p>
