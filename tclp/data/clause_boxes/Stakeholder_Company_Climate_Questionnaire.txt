	<h4>Stakeholder Company Climate Questionnaire</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A questionnaire for a third party to put to a company or other organisation to find out more about its taking decarbonisation or net-zero transition plan. A questionnaire for third parties (for example, buyers, suppliers and employees) to elicit information from a company or other organisation about its climate ambition, decarbonisation or net-zero transition plan.</p>
