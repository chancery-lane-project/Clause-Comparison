	<h4>Low Carbon Arbitrations</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Clauses encouraging parties to arbitration to create all documents in electronic form and conduct all hearings remotely/virtually, where possible. Drives sustainable practice in arbitration and reduces greenhouse gas emitting behaviours in proceedings.</p>
