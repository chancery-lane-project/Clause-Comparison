Child's Name: Lewi's Clause
Long Name: Film Production: Carbon Footprint Disclosure
Excerpt: A clause in production agreements obliging video/film production companies to disclose the carbon footprint generated by the production of the video/film in the credits. Encourages parties to film/ video production agreements to take positive action to reduce the carbon footprint of the production, and to then publicise this in the credits to raise public awareness of the need to actively reduce greenhouse gas emissions in all spheres of economic activity.
