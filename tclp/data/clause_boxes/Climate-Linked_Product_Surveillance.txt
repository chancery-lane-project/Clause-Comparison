	<h4>Climate-Linked Product Surveillance</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A clause for supply contracts incentivising climate change adaptation in product supply chains. This clause creates an express mechanism under which contracting parties – such as a manufacturer and retailer or a supplier and a distributor – are required to assess and respond to the impacts of climate change on product safety. This clause is intended to make contracting parties think about climate change resilience and, in doing so, foster greater climate awareness.</p>
