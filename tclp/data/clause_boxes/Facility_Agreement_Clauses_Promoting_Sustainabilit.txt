	<h4>Facility Agreement Clauses Promoting Sustainability-Linked Loans within the Cement Industry</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">A set of sustainability-linked clauses to be incorporated into Facility Agreements: including covenants, representations and a ratcheted interest rate linked to emissions reduction performance. Sets sustainability targets in loans to actors in the high emitting cement industry to incentivise them to achieve dramatic reductions in emissions.</p>
