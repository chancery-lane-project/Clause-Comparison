Child's Name: Marni's Clause
Long Name: Report on Title – Climate Change Clauses
Excerpt: This clause proposes additions to standard reports on title to reflect the results of a climate search for the property. Improves transparency and reporting about the physical, transition and future liability risks of climate change for properties in the UK
This clause also helps solicitors fulfil their duty of care to advise clients as to the presence of climate change risks, as outlined in the Law Society (England and Wales) Guidance: The impact of climate change on solicitors.
