	<h4>CLLS Certificate of Title: Climate Change Disclosures</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Voluntary disclosures to the standard form CLLS Certificate of Title to update investors on environmental and climate disclosures that may affect the long term value of the property. The City of London Law Society (CLLS) Certificate of Title has become the standard form of title report for the majority of transactions involving real estate finance. These disclosures can be used on a voluntary basis until such time as the Certificate of Title is updated to address and incorporate them.</p>
