Child's Name: Annie's Clause
Long Name: Green Termination Provision (Short Form)
Excerpt: A clause that allows a right of termination for a customer so that they can pivot to a greener supplier to meet their sustainability, climate or other environmental objectives. Gives customers a right to switch suppliers if the existing supplier is unable to match a ‘greener’ offer from an alternative supplier. This embeds assessment of green credentials into procurement, supporting parties to meet their net zero targets and increase their competitiveness.
