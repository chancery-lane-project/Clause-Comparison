	<h4>Supply: Termination for Greener Supplier</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">The clause gives customers a right to switch supplier if their existing supplier is unable to match a ‘greener’ offer made by an alternative supplier. This clause gives a customer the right to terminate an agreement with an existing supplier if it can get the goods or services with a lower carbon footprint from an alternative supplier.</p>
