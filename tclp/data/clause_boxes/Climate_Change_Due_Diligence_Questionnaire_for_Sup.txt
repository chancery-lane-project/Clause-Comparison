Child's Name: Raphael’s Procurement DDQ
Long Name: Climate Change Due Diligence Questionnaire for Suppliers
Excerpt: A due diligence questionnaire (DDQ) that asks potential suppliers to provide information regarding a wide range of climate change-related issues going beyond the standard questions. This DDQ provides climate-related questions which will enable procurement teams to appraise supplier approaches to climate-related risks and impacts (if at all) and how well they align with the customer’s own climate strategy and targets.
