	<h4>SPA/ Investment Agreement: Disclosure of Climate Change Plans</h4>
	<p class="excerpt">Including pro-active disclosure requirements in M&A or investment transactions brings climate targets and aims to the forefront of the minds of management of the target and the buyer/ investors. Enables a buyer to gauge the climate risk inherent in a target through climate warranties and disclosures.</p>
