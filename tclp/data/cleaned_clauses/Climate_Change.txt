Climate Change means a change in the state of the climate that can be identified and that persists for[an extended period of time OR decades]and where changes in the composition of the global atmosphere is caused directly or indirectly by an accumulated increase in Scope 1, 2 and 3 anthropogenic Greenhouse Gas Emissions, which is in addition to natural climate variability.
