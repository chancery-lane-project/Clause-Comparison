2 Carbon Capture Storage(CCS)means the use of technology to safely:(a)capture waste carbon dioxide at the emissions source; (b)transport the captured carbon dioxide; and(c)[either use or]permanently store the captured carbon dioxide such that the captured carbon dioxide is not released into the atmosphere.

Carbon Capture Storage(CCS)OR[Carbon Capture Usage and Storage(CCUS)]means the[technology for and]process of capturing, transporting[and using or]storing waste carbon dioxide.
