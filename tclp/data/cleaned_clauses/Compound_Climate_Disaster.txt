Compound Climate Disaster means:(a)two or more extreme weather or climate events occurring simultaneously or successively; (b)combinations of extreme weather or climate events with underlying conditions that amplify the impact of those events; or(c)combinations of weather or climate events that are not themselves extremes but lead to an extreme weather or climate event or impact when combined.
