1 Alternative Fuel means fuel or power sources used as a substitute for fossil oil sources in transport.

They have the potential to help to decarbonise the transport sector and enhance its Environmental Performance and include:(a)electricity; (b)hydrogen; (c)liquid or gaseous biofuels derived from biomass; (d)synthetic and paraffinic fuels; (e)natural gas, including biomethane in gaseous form(compressed natural gas)and liquefied form(liquefied natural gas(LNG)); and(f)liquefied petroleum gas.
